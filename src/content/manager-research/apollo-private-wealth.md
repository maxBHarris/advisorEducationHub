## Overview

Apollo's Private Wealth Solutions platform provides financial advisors and individual investors with access to institutional-quality alternative investments with a focus on credit-oriented strategies. With over $650 billion in assets under management, Apollo combines credit expertise with opportunistic investing across private markets.

## About Apollo Private Wealth

Apollo is a leading global alternative investment manager founded in 1990, specializing in credit-oriented and opportunistic strategies across private equity, credit, and real assets. The firm is known for its deep analytical capabilities, complex deal structuring, and credit-first investment philosophy.

### Key Achievements

- **$650+ Billion AUM**: One of the world's largest alternative asset managers
- **30+ Years**: Track record across credit cycles and market environments
- **Global Platform**: Investment professionals across major financial centers worldwide
- **Credit Expertise**: Industry-leading capabilities in credit analysis and workout situations

## Investment Solutions

### Apollo Debt Solutions BDC

**Asset Class**: Private Credit

Apollo Debt Solutions BDC provides access to U.S. direct lending opportunities through a business development company structure.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Business Development Company (BDC)
- **Investment Strategy**: Direct lending to middle-market borrowers
- **Focus**: Senior secured floating rate loans
- **Liquidity**: Publicly traded structure

### Apollo Asset Backed Credit Company

**Asset Class**: Private Credit (Asset-Backed)

Specialized focus on asset-backed lending and specialty finance strategies.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Business Development Company (BDC)
- **Investment Strategy**: Asset-backed lending and specialty finance
- **Focus**: Asset-secured lending opportunities
- **Differentiation**: Specialized collateral expertise

### Apollo Diversified Credit Fund

**Asset Class**: Private Credit (Multi-Strategy)

Diversified exposure across Apollo's credit platform and strategies.

**Key Features:**
