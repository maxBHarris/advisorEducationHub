## Overview

Blackstone's Private Wealth Solutions platform provides financial advisors and wealth management professionals with access to institutional-quality alternative investments across private equity, private credit, real estate, and infrastructure. As the world's largest alternative asset manager with over $1 trillion in assets under management, Blackstone has pioneered making institutional strategies accessible to individual investors.

## About Blackstone Private Wealth

Blackstone is the world's largest alternative asset manager and a global leader in private equity, credit, real estate, infrastructure, and hedge fund solutions. Founded in 1985, the firm has built a track record spanning nearly four decades across market cycles.

## Investment Solutions

### BREIT - Blackstone Real Estate Income Trust

**Asset Class**: Real Estate

BREIT provides individual investors access to Blackstone's institutional real estate platform through a non-traded REIT structure. The fund invests in income-producing properties across industrial, multifamily, hospitality, and other sectors.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Non-Traded REIT with monthly NAV
- **Investment Strategy**: Diversified portfolio of institutional-quality real estate
- **Liquidity**: Monthly redemption requests (subject to quarterly limits)
- **AUM**: $50+ billion
- **Focus**: Income generation with capital appreciation potential

### BCRED - Blackstone Private Credit BDC

**Asset Class**: Private Credit

BCRED offers access to Blackstone's senior secured lending platform through a publicly-traded business development company (BDC) structure.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Business Development Company (BDC) trading on NYSE
- **Investment Strategy**: Senior secured loans to U.S. middle-market companies
- **Liquidity**: Daily trading on public exchange
- **Focus**: Current income through floating rate loans

### BXPE - Blackstone Private Equity Strategies Fund

**Asset Class**: Private Equity

BXPE provides individual investors access to Blackstone's private equity platform through an interval fund structure, including primary funds, co-investments, and secondaries.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Interval Fund
- **Investment Strategy**: Diversified private equity portfolio with co-investments and secondaries
- **Liquidity**: Quarterly redemption windows
- **Focus**: Long-term capital appreciation

### BXINFRA - Blackstone Infrastructure Strategies Fund

**Asset Class**: Infrastructure

BXINFRA offers access to Blackstone's infrastructure investment platform, focusing on essential assets with predictable cash flows.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Interval Fund
- **Investment Strategy**: Diversified infrastructure across energy, transportation, and digital infrastructure
- **Liquidity**: Quarterly redemption windows
- **Focus**: Income generation with inflation protection

### BMACX - Blackstone Multi-Asset Credit Interval Fund

**Asset Class**: Private Credit (Multi-Strategy)

BMACX provides diversified credit exposure across multiple credit strategies through an interval fund structure.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Interval Fund
- **Investment Strategy**: Diversified credit portfolio including direct lending, high yield, and opportunistic credit
- **Liquidity**: Quarterly redemptions
- **Focus**: Income generation across credit spectrum
