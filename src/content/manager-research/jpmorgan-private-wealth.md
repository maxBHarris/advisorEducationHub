## Overview

JP Morgan Asset Management's Private Wealth Solutions provides financial advisors and individual investors with access to institutional-quality alternative investments across private equity, private credit, real estate, and infrastructure. Backed by JPMorgan Chase's global resources and over $2.5 trillion in assets under management, JP Morgan combines investment expertise with comprehensive wealth management capabilities.

## About JP Morgan Asset Management

JP Morgan Asset Management is one of the world's leading investment managers and part of JPMorgan Chase & Co., a global financial services firm with over 200 years of history. The firm provides sophisticated investment strategies and portfolio construction expertise across public and private markets.

## Investment Solutions

### JP Morgan Private Markets Fund

**Asset Class**: Private Equity / Multi-Strategy

The JP Morgan Private Markets Fund provides individual investors access to diversified private markets exposure through a registered fund structure.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Registered fund with institutional backing
- **Investment Strategy**: Diversified private markets portfolio construction
- **Access**: Combines primary funds, secondaries, and co-investments
- **Expertise**: Leverages JP Morgan's global private equity team
- **Focus**: Long-term capital appreciation through private company ownership
