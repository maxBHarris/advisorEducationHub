## Overview

AMG Pantheon combines AMG's distribution expertise and Pantheon's institutional private equity capabilities to provide financial advisors and individual investors with access to diversified private markets investments. Through an evergreen fund-of-funds structure, AMG Pantheon offers exposure to multiple private equity managers, strategies, and vintage years in a single investment.

## Investment Solution

### AMG Pantheon Fund (PPEX) - Class I

**Asset Class**: Private Equity (Fund-of-Funds)

The AMG Pantheon Fund provides individual investors access to institutional-quality private equity through a diversified fund-of-funds approach in an evergreen registered fund structure.

**Key Features:**
- **Structure**: Evergreen registered fund (continuously offered)
- **Investment Strategy**: Diversified fund-of-funds approach investing across:
  - Primary private equity funds
  - Secondary market opportunities
  - Co-investment opportunities
- **Diversification**: Exposure to multiple PE managers, strategies, geographies, and vintage years
- **Vintage Year Diversification**: Ongoing capital deployment reduces vintage year concentration risk
- **Liquidity**: Evergreen structure provides more flexibility than traditional closed-end funds
- **Minimum Investment**: Varies by share class (contact for current requirements)

**Investment Approach:**

The fund-of-funds structure provides several benefits:

1. **Manager Diversification**: Access to multiple best-in-class PE managers that may have high minimums or be closed to new investors
2. **Strategy Diversification**: Exposure across buyout, growth equity, and other PE strategies
3. **Geographic Diversification**: Global exposure across developed and emerging markets
4. **Vintage Year Management**: Continuous investment smooths out vintage year risk
5. **Access to Co-Investments**: Leverage Pantheon's relationships for lower-fee co-investment opportunities
