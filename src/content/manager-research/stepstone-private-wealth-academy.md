## Overview

StepStone Group's Private Wealth Academy is a comprehensive educational platform designed to equip financial advisors and wealth management professionals with foundational knowledge about private markets and alternative investments. The Academy provides on-demand continuing education credits, videos, white papers, and extensive resources for advisors of all experience levels.

## About StepStone Private Wealth Solutions

StepStone Private Wealth Solutions has experienced significant growth, recently surpassing **$10.2 billion in assets under management** after adding $5 billion in under one year. The firm is focused on democratizing access to private markets by partnering with financial advisors, RIAs, retail wholesalers, and wirehouses globally.

### Key Achievements

- **$10.2B+ AUM**: Doubled assets under management in record time
- **Global Expansion**: Growing partnerships across international markets
- **Educational Leadership**: Pioneering advisor education in private markets
- **Accessibility Focus**: Making institutional-quality alternatives available to a broader investor base

## Educational Offerings

### On-Demand Continuing Education

The StepStone Academy offers continuing education credits that financial professionals can earn by:

1. **Registering** for courses on the platform
2. **Watching** educational videos and content
3. **Completing** knowledge assessments
4. **Earning** credits to further their career development

### Core Topics Covered

#### Private Equity and Venture Capital

Learn the fundamentals of how these vehicles function, including:

- **Investment Structure**: Understanding fund mechanics and lifecycle
- **Portfolio Fit**: Where PE/VC fits in a diversified investment strategy
- **Return Potential**: Why these assets are central to private markets due to their return characteristics
- **Active Ownership Model**: How PE managers create value through operational improvements

#### Private Markets Fundamentals

- Asset class overview and comparison
- Risk-return profiles across different alternative strategies
- Portfolio construction and allocation strategies
- Due diligence and manager selection
- Performance measurement and benchmarking

### Resource Types Available

- **Video Content**: On-demand educational videos covering key topics
- **White Papers**: In-depth research and analysis documents
- **Webinars**: Live and recorded sessions with industry experts
- **Case Studies**: Real-world examples and investment scenarios

The Academy is designed for:

- **Financial Advisors**: RIAs and independent advisors seeking to expand their alternative investment expertise
- **Retail Wholesalers**: Distribution professionals educating advisors about private markets
- **Wirehouses**: Large brokerage firms offering alternatives to clients
- **Wealth Management Teams**: Multi-advisor practices building alternatives capabilities
